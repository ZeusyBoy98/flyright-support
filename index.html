FlyRight is the best flight simulator companion app.
Go to the logbook page to create and view your logs.
Press the plus icon in the top right to create a log
On the create log page, you can fill out the title, departure and arrival airports, set the date, the duration, and right a comment. Press the calendar to get a date picker.
Once you create a log, you can select it from the logbook page to view it.
Go to the checklist page to create, view, and interact with your checklists.
Press the plus icon in the top right to create a checklist
On the create checklist page, you can fill out the title, choose the checklist colour, and add infinite items.
Press on the palette to open the colour picker.
Once you create a checklist, you can select it from the checklists page to view, and interact with it.
You can easily press on an item to tick it off, and its ticked or unticked state is saved, even when you exit the checklist or close the app, until you clear the checklist.
On the convert page, you can easily convert between commonly used aviation units.
Press on the arrows to change the conversion direction.
